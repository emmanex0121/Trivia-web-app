ABOUT TRIVIA GURU

Welcome to Trivia Guru, the ultimate web app for quiz enthusiasts! Dive into an exciting world of knowledge and challenge your brain with our diverse and engaging trivia questions. Powered by the Open Trivia Database (OpenTDB), Trivia Guru offers a seamless and entertaining way to test your wits across a wide range of topics.


What We Offer

  • Instant Play: Jump right into the action with our Play Now feature. With just a click, you can start answering questions from a random mix of categories and difficulty levels. It"s perfect for those looking for a quick and fun trivia fix!
  • Mode Select: Prefer a more tailored experience? Our Mode Select page lets you choose from a broad array of categories and three difficulty levels. Whether you're a history buff, a science geek, or just looking for some general knowledge fun, Trivia Guru has something for everyone.

Why Trivia Guru? Trivia Guru is designed to be intuitive, user-friendly, and engaging for trivia lovers of all ages. By pulling questions from OpenTDB, we ensure a constantly updated and diverse question pool, so you can enjoy a fresh and challenging experience every time you play. Test your knowledge, learn something new, and become the ultimate trivia master with Trivia Guru!
